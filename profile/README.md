# Hazen Solutions

Hazen Solutions is a software engineering company focused on building maintainable, scalable, and reusable software.

Our mission is to build an engineering platform where every project contributes back to the company through reusable modules, templates, tooling, automation, and documentation.

---

## Engineering Philosophy

We believe in building platforms, not just projects.

Every project should leave the engineering platform better than before.

Our core principles include:

- Simplicity over Complexity
- Readability First
- Consistency over Preference
- Documentation is Part of Development
- Automation Over Manual Work
- Build Platform, Not Projects

---

## Technology

Our primary stack includes:

- Laravel
- Blade
- Tailwind CSS

Additional technologies are adopted only when they provide the best solution for a specific problem.

---

## Engineering Platform

Our long-term vision is to build a reusable engineering platform consisting of:

```text
Platform
    ↓
Modules
    ↓
Templates
    ↓
Projects
```

Business logic belongs to projects.

Reusable assets belong to the platform.
